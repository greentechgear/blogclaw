# Traffic Analysis Log

Automated referral traffic analysis powered by BlogClaw + Clicky.

## How to Read This Log

- **Referral Categories**: Where your traffic comes from (social, search, community, direct links)
- **Trending Articles**: Content gaining momentum — prioritize promotion
- **Engagement Recommendations**: Specific actions to take based on traffic patterns
- **Patterns**: Recurring trends across multiple analysis periods

## Recommendation Priority Levels

- 🔴 **High** — Active channel already producing results. Act now to amplify.
- 🟡 **Medium** — Promising signal worth investing time in.
- 🟢 **Low** — Exploratory. Test when you have bandwidth.

## Referral Categories Explained

| Category | What It Means | Example Sources |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Social | Social media platforms | Reddit, Twitter/X, LinkedIn |
| Search | Organic search engines | Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo |
| Community | Developer/tech communities | Hacker News, Dev.to, Lobsters |
| Aggregator | RSS readers & content apps | Feedly, Pocket, Flipboard |
| Direct Referral | Other websites linking to you | Blog posts, articles, docs |
